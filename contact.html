Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about visiting Paris

What's the best time to visit Paris?

The best times to visit Paris are spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) when the weather is mild and crowds are smaller. Summer offers long days but can be crowded, while winter provides a magical atmosphere with fewer tourists.

Do I need to book attraction tickets in advance?

Yes, we strongly recommend booking tickets online in advance for major attractions like the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Versailles. This helps you avoid long queues and ensures entry, especially during peak tourist seasons.

Is it necessary to speak French in Paris?

While many Parisians in tourist areas speak English, learning basic French phrases like "Bonjour" (hello) and "Merci" (thank you) is greatly appreciated. Making an effort to speak French, even poorly, often results in warmer interactions.
